Zebrafish water tank model (ZWT): a tool for human-like metabolism studies

Zebrafish (Danio rerio) water tank (ZWT) approach was investigated as an alternative model for metabolism studies, based on six different experiments with four model compounds. Also, we were interested on the impact of steroid hormones on the endogenous metabolism of zebrafish. We discuss the appropriate conditions, merits, and relevant limitations to conduct ZWT experiments for the investigation of drug metabolism.
